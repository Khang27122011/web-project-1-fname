About KFC

KFC is one of the world's largest fast-food chains specializing in fried chicken. Founded by Colonel Harland Sanders, it has become famous for its secret 11 herbs and spices recipe.

KFC has over 24,000 restaurants worldwide and continues to expand. It offers a variety of chicken meals to suit different tastes.

Eating unhealthy isn't always fun, but KFC also offers grilled options for healthier choices.

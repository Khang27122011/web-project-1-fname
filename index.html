About Us

Founded in 1930 by Colonel Harland Sanders in Kentucky, KFC has become one of the largest fast-food chains worldwide, famous for its signature Original Recipe chicken made with a secret blend of 11 herbs and spices.

We serve millions of customers through over 24,000 restaurants in more than 145 countries.
